The heavy rains and related calamities have forced the Kerala Road Transport Corporation to stop several services. However, Kerala RTC has ensured that as many services as possible are kept running.
The Kerala road transport corporation has released an official list of numbers to help passengers. A message circulated by the official social media team of the state transporter requested the passengers to avoid any unnecessary travel and said the passengers must confirm the services before they start any journey.
