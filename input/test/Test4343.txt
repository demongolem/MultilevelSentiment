Written by: NEWS DIVISION August 16, 2018
Sega Games Co., Ltd. this week released the Combat Trailer for Fist of the North Star: Lost Paradise for Sony Corp.'s PlayStation 4.
The trailer previews brutal combat techniques from the final game.
Developed by Ryu ga Gotoku, Fist of the North Star: Lost Paradise is a third-person action RPG title based on the classic manga.
The final game will include Kenshiro in an alternate universe of the original story, Hokuto Shinken techniques and a customizable buggy.
It will be sold Oct. 2
