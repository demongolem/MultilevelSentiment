About This Game Immerse yourself in this super fun soccer goalkeeper simulator! You can test your skills in four different tournaments: - FA Cup - Champions League - World Cup You can start off by competing in the relatively easy FA Cup to hone your goalkeeping skills and improve your hand reflexes. Or if you're confident enough you can dive straight into the more challenging tournaments like the Champions League or World Cup! You can play for the club that you've always supported! Or perhaps don the goalkeeping jersey of your national team and achieve your boyhood dream of bringing home the World Cup System Requirement
