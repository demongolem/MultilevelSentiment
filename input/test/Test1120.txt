Early voting for the Primary Election in Escambia County begins throughout the county. There are eight locations for early voting that will be open from 9am until 6pm: The Supervisor of Elections Office, 213 Palafox Place in downtown Pensacola The Main Library at Spring and Gregory The Genealogy Branch Library at 9 th Avenue and College Boulevard The Southwest Branch Library, 12248 Gulf Beach Highway The Mobile Highway-Pine Forest Road Early Voting Center, 6675 Pine Forest Road The Brownsville Community Center, 3200 West DeSoto Street The Escambia County Extension building, 3740 Stefani Road The Molino Community Center, 6450-A Highway 95-A. \\
Early voting ends Saturday, August 25th and voters are reminded to bring their photo and signature ID with them to the polls
