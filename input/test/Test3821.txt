Social media addiction leads to health issues 854 views Aug 17, 2018, 09:30 IST Social media addiction leads to health issues Aug 17, 2018, 09:30 IST Source: TNN Social Media has become a behemoth in today's world that has grown by leaps and bounds in the last few years.More than a quarter of the world's population equipped with an internet connection and a phone, is on Facebook, Whatsapp, Instagram and Twitter. This has led to damage to our thumb muscles due to an incessant use of the thumb to text and operate cellular phones. One can also face carpal tunnel syndrome, which damages hands or wrists due to typing too much. A prolonged use of smartphones can also increase neck and back pain. Even adolescents and teens suffer from problems from these problems related to neck, back and wrist pain due to smartphone usage
