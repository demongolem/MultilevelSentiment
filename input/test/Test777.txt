PSBank advisory: System upgrade to affect ATM-related services ABS-CBN News Posted at Aug 17 2018 03:36 PM
Share Facebook
MANILA - PSBank on Friday said its ATM-related services will temporarily be unavailable on Aug 20 due to a system upgrade.
Cardless withdrawal and other ATM transactions won't be availabe from 1:00 a.m. to 8:00 a.m. on Monday, Aug. 20, PSBank said in an advisory.
The bank said all mobile and online channels will be available.
Clients were advised to call the bank's hotline 8458888 for concerns.
