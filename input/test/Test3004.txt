SLA Digital partners with Mobile Arts to launch direct carrier billing 11:30 CET | News Mobile Arts, a technology provider in the Middle East, has partnered with SLA Digital to expand its revenues throught Direct Carrier Billing for its premium gaming and utility applications. SLA Digital says it continues to work with Mobile Arts on expanding its connectivity through SLA Digital's portfolio of mobile operators in the Middle East and Asia.
