Airtel supports floods relief efforts in Kerala 46 CET | News Airtel has announced a series of measures to help people affected by the recent floods in Kerala. Airtel will offer auto approval of 'Talk Time' credit up to INR 30 for all its prepay mobile customers in the region. Airtel will also offer free 1 GB mobile data for all Airtel prepaid smartphone users (data valid for seven days); as well as an extension of bill payment dates for all Airtel postpaid and home broadband customers to ensure they have uninterrupted access to services.
