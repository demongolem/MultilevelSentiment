Gracenote launches new Mobile Video Analytics platform 12:43 CET | News Gracenote, a Nielsen company, launched a new Mobile Video Analytics platform aimed at providing visibility into the quality and performance of the most popular mobile video streaming applications. The performance analytics platform will provide mobile operators, MVPDs, video streaming services and hardware manufacturers a means to better understand the ways in which mobile app performance impacts user behavior and attitudes as well as engagement with their platforms and those of their competitors
