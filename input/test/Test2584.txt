Optus deploys mobile tower in Jindera 11:29 CET | News Australian operator Optus has deployed a mobile tower in the town of Jindera. The new tower, designed to upgrade mobile reception and cater for floodlights in the recreation reserve, will address a known black spot identified by the local community, providing them with dedicated mobile reception to make and receive calls, SMS and access mobile data in more places.
