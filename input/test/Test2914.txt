The Product Owner represents the voice of stakeholders by maintaining and prioritizing the backlog to ensure that engineering teams are working on stories that maximize business value and deliver necessary fixes and functionality to internal stakeholders. The ideal candidate will be one with prior product management experience who leads by example, can communicate in terms of value, and can identify aggregate feature sets that progress technical and business goals. Possess a fundamental understanding of end-to-end customer experience integration and dependencies. Collaborate with stakeholders (cross-functional teams, business partners, management) during the visioning and concept development of a client project. Assess value, prioritize and write stories, epics and themes to ensure work focuses on those with maximum value that are aligned with product strategy. Collaborate with internal stakeholders to translate business and technical requirements into user and technical stories for Engineering scrum teams to implement. Create and maintain the Product backlog. Attend sprint planning meetings. Present on product and component progress to stakeholders and leadership. In-depth understanding of Agile methodologies, technical understanding of products, and up-to-date on industry standards and best practices.
