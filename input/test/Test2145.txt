3 UK launches new TV ad campaign focused on travel benefits 11:03 CET | News 3 UK has unveiled a new above-the-line (ATL) campaign to advertise the travel benefits available to its customers, which include a no-charge upgrade to EasyJet's Hands Free service. As announced at the end of July, the operator signed a two-year partnership with the low-cost airline to offer a number of benefits to its customers travelling from the UK with EasyJet.
