New Delhi [India], Aug 17 (ANI): Bollywood actress Taapsee Pannu unveiled the lyrical video of the title track from her upcoming movie 'Manmarziyaan' and the melodious number is all about the essence and feeling of first love.
The 'Pink' actress took to Twitter and shared the video, writing, "Kar Lae Manmarziyaan JaisiTeriMarzi! Title Track out
now"
The new song, titled 'Jaisi Teri Marzi', is sung by Harshdeep Kaur and Bhanu Pratap Singh, and composed by Amit Trivedi. The lyrics have been penned by Shellee.
'Manmarziyaan' will see Abhishek Bachchan return to the big screen after a gap of two years since he was last seen in the 2016 rom-com 'Housefull 3'.
