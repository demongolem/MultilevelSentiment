Stella-Jones (SJ) Price Target Raised to C$55.00  Anthony Miller | Aug 17th, 2018
Stella-Jones (TSE:SJ) had its target price upped by National Bank Financial from C$53.00 to C$55.00 in a research report sent to investors on Tuesday morning. The firm currently has an outperform rating on the stock. National Bank Financial also issued estimates for Stella-Jones' Q3 2018 earnings at $0.65 EPS, Q4 2018 earnings at $0.51 EPS and FY2018 earnings at $2.19 EPS.
Several other research firms have also issued reports on SJ. Laurentian upped their target price on shares of Stella-Jones from C$50.00 to C$52.00 in a research note on Friday, May 4th. TD Securities upped their target price on shares of Stella-Jones from C$53.00 to C$54.00 and gave the stock a buy rating in a research note on Friday, May 4th. CIBC reissued an outperform rating and issued a C$55.00 price target on shares of Stella-Jones in a report on Tuesday, June 5th. Finally, Desjardins boosted their price target on shares of Stella-Jones from C$50.00 to C$51.00 in a report on Friday, May 4th. One analyst has rated the stock with a hold rating and seven have assigned a buy rating to the company. Stella-Jones presently has an average rating of Buy and a consensus target price of C$52.50. About Stella-Jones
Stella-Jones Inc produces, markets, and sells pressure treated wood products in Canada and the United States. It offers railway ties and timbers for railroad operators; and utility poles for electrical utilities and telecommunication companies. The company also provides residential lumber and accessories to retailers and wholesalers for use in decks, fences, patios, and other outdoor applications; and industrial products, including bridge timbers and docks, foundation and marine pilings, highway guardrail posts, and panelized railway crossings; and construction timbers.
