Ferrari World Abu Dhabi today joined theme parks across the world in marking National Rollercoaster Day, an occasion in which the biggest theme parks and amusement destinations in the world pay homage to the exciting rollercoaster, rides and attractions on offer at their parks.
In addition to hosting a series of engaging activations for guests to enjoy, the Ferrari World Abu Dhabi team took the opportunity to share interesting facts on the region's leading theme park's winning mix of rides and attractions, encouraging guests to participate in thrilling rollercoaster-related challenges that tested their mettle onboard the park's record-breaking rollercoasters Formula Rossa, Flying Aces, Turbo Track and Fiorano GT Challenge. Ferrari is an Italian sports car manufacturer based in Maranello, Italy. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, the company sponsored drivers and manufactured race cars before moving into production of street-legal vehicles as Ferrari S.p.A. in 1947. Throughout its history, the company has been noted for its continued participation in racing, especially in Formula One, where it has had great success.
Ferrari Middle East & Africa is based in Dubai. The company provides services and support to its authorized dealer network with Sales and After-Sales, Customer Service, PR and Marketing activities in the following nine markets: Bahrain, Egypt, Kuwait, Lebanon, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, South-Africa, UAE
