BRISTOL - Amazon is hosting a walk-in job application event today at DoubleTree by Hilton.
Amazon will be opening a delivery station in Bristol later this month.
Interested candidates for jobs with the online retailing giant can go to 42 Century Drive from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. and fill out an application.
They should bring information needed to complete the I-9 employment eligibility form, and give consent to background checks and drug testing.
