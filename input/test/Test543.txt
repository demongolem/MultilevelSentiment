Westcon Sub-Saharan Africa signs distribution deal with Extreme Networks Friday 17 August 2018 | 10:47 CET | News Westcon Sub-Saharan Africa said that is has secured the sole rights to distribute Extreme Networks in Kenya, Mauritius, Nigeria, Ethiopia, Tanzania and Angola. Resellers in those countries will now be able to fulfil their Extreme Networks requirements and offer customers access to the vendor's enterprise networking services.
