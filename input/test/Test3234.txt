German regulator doubtful over legal basis to impose national roaming 11:53 CET | News Germany's Federal Network Agency (BNetzA) has expressed doubts over the necessity of new regulations to support the launch of a fourth German mobile network, reported Golem.de. The regulator has "considerable legal concerns" over the proposed national mobile roaming conditions that a new entrant like United Internet would need to enter the German mobile market, wrote Golem.
