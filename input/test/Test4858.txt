TORONTO , Aug. 17, 2018 /PRNewswire/ - Symbility Solutions Inc. ("Symbility") (TSX.V: SY), a global software company focused on modernizing the insurance industry, confirmed that it will release its second quarter financial results for the three months ended June 30, 2018 , before the market opens on Friday, August 24, 2018 .
