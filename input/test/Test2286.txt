NEW DELHI: Shares of Motherson Sumi Systems (up 2.38 per cent), Bharat Forge (up 2.23 per cent) and Tata Motors (up 2.21 per cent) jumped over 2 per cent in Friday's session, pulling the BSE Auto index in the green.
The BSE Auto index was trading 0.52 per cent up at 24,397 around 02:10 pm.
Ashok Leyland (up 1.90 per cent), Cummins India (up 1.41 per cent), Apollo Tyres (up 1.23 per cent) and Mahindra & Mahindra (up 1.04 per cent) too were trading with decent gains.
MRF (up 0.51 per cent), Bosch (up 0.28 per cent) and Exide Industries (up 0.14 per cent) were in the green.
However, Balkrishna Industries (down 1.44 per cent), TVS Motor Company (down 0.61 per cent), Maruti Suzuki India (down 0.38 per cent), Hero MotoCorp (down 0.31 per cent), Bajaj Auto (down 0.23 per cent) and Eicher Motors (down 0.22 per cent) were in the red around that time.
Benchmark BSE Sensex was up 302 points at 37,966, while the NSE Nifty50 index was up 88 points at 11,473.
Among the 50 stocks in the Nifty index, 37 were trading in the green, while 13 were in the red.
In the Sensex index, 23 stocks were advancing and 8 were declining.
