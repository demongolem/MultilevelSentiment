The Haridwar-based firm has now put Kimbho's trial version on Google Play Store for downloads and plans to launch the app officially on August 27. It will also be available on Apple App store shortly.
Patanjali spokesperson S K Tijarawala told PTI : "We have put the trial version of Kimbho on the google play store, so that we come to know about the shortcoming. We are open for any amendments for any errors and we have invited feedback from the people also". According to him, this will be a "safe, secure and swadeshi" messaging platform. The company has also claimed that Kimbho had witnessed around 1.5 lakh downloads in the first three hours when its trial version was earlier put on Google Play Store and App Store for a day only
