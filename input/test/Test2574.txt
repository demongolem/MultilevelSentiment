Rovio Q2 sales slide on unfavourable comparison effect, weak dollar 11:18 CET | News Finland-based Rovio's revenues were EUR 71.8 million in the three months to end-June, down 16.8 percent from EUR 86.2 million a year earlier, with depreciation of the US dollar accounting for around EUR 9 million of the decline.
