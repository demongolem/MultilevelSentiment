3 UK announces pricing for new Samsung Galaxy Note 9 17 CET | News 3 UK has followed its mobile rivals in announcing the pricing of the new Samsung Galaxy Note 9 smartphone, ahead of its launch on 24 August. Now accepting advance orders, the operator is offering the handset for an upfront cost of GBP 79 with selected Advanced plans, from GBP 48 per month on a 24-month contract, and with no upfront cost with its GBP 91.00 all-you-can-eat data plan
