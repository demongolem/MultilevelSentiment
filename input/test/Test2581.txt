Base adds unlimited calls for EUR 5 on entry-level postpaid plan 11:02 CET | News Belgian operator Base has created a new entry-level postpaid plan at EUR 20 a month. Customers taking its Based on you 15 plan, with 3 GB of data and 300 minutes for EUR 15 per month, can now add unlimited calls to all networks for an extra EUR 5 per month.
